WESTPA Workshop, August 2-3, 2018

This year's WESTPA workshop will be held at the University of Pittsburgh. Registration is free, but participants are responsible for covering the costs of transportation and accommodation. Rooms at the Wyndham Hotel (one block from the workshop location) can be reserved before July 11th at the University of Pittsburgh discount rate of $139/day using the following link:
https://www.wyndhamhotels.com/groups/hr/westpa
If you would like to arrive outside the group arrival dates you can contact the following staff at the hotel directly.
Mara Cerro 412-682-6251 or email mcerro@wyndham.com; Gabby Gassman 412-682-6200 ext. 6131 ggassman@wyndham.com
The first day, Aug 2, will consist of lectures as well as tutorials. The optional second day, Aug 3, will be an opportunity for one-on-one consultations with WESTPA developers.
To enable us to hit the ground running during the workshop, we ask that participants complete one of the introductory WESTPA tutorials linked below before attending the workshop. THIS IS ABSOLUTELY ESSENTIAL AND SHOULD BE CONSIDERED A REQUIREMENT.
https://github.com/westpa/westpa/wiki/WESTPA-Tutorials#Introductory_Tutorials
